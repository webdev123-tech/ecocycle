The Problem

Kampala is drowning in its own waste

2,500twaste generated every day
~48%never collected
35+killed, Kiteezi collapse 2024

On 9 August 2024, heavy rain collapsed the overfull Kiteezi landfill — burying homes and killing dozens. It had been past capacity since 2012, yet still took 1,500–2,000 tonnes a day.

EcoCycle2
Root Cause

It's a broken information loop

Citizens can't report dumps. Collectors work blind, with no job lists or proof. Cities have no real-time data. And nobody is rewarded for recycling — so recyclables get buried.

EcoCycle reconnects all three.

The Solution

One app, three roles, one loop

👤

Citizen

Report waste with photo + GPS, get AI sorting, schedule & pay for pickups, earn rewards.

🚛

Collector

Live job queue, navigation, photo proof-of-collection, live truck location.

🏙️

City / Admin

Live map of reports, emergency alerts, and an SDG impact dashboard.
